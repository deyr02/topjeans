                          Project Details

                          Top jeans is a fictional company. This is the part of my portfolio project to demonstrate various skills on web development.

                          Projcect Scope

                          Top jeans has multiple store across New Zealand and Australia. Recently, the company has decided to build an online platform to sell product globally. Initially, the company has decided to build a simple pilot project to make sure that their customer stay tuned and updated with “Top Jeans”. The main purpose of the pilot project is to show product, implement product filter according to categories, gender and brand, implement shopping cart, save user’s favourite item, and change currency etc.
